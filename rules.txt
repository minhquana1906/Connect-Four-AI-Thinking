1. Connect 4 is a two-player game where players take
   turns dropping colored discs into a vertical grid.

2. The objective is to be the first to form a horizontal,
   vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own discs.

3. Player 1 uses RED discs and Player 2/AI uses 
    YELLOW discs.

HOW TO PLAY:

1. Move your mouse to select a column.
2. Click to drop your disc into that column.
3. Try to connect four of your discs while preventing 
your opponent from connecting four.
